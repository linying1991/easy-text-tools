Frequently Asked Questions

What phone number formats are supported?

The tool works with 10-digit US numbers (e.g., 1234567890) and 11-digit numbers with country code 1 (e.g., 11234567890). It automatically detects the format and applies appropriate styling.

How do I format international phone numbers?

This tool is optimized for US phone numbers. For international numbers, enter the full number including country code, and it will display it in a basic international format.

Can I copy formatted phone numbers?

Yes, click on any formatted phone number in the results list to copy it to your clipboard. A confirmation message will appear when copying is successful.

Are my phone numbers stored or transmitted?

No, all processing happens locally in your browser. Your phone numbers are not stored, transmitted, or shared with any server, ensuring complete privacy.
