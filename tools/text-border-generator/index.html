Frequently Asked Questions

What border styles are available?

The tool provides four decorative border styles: Box borders with rounded corners, Star borders with asterisks, Double-line borders with double-width characters, and ASCII Art borders with simple plus and dash characters.

How are multi-line texts handled?

Multi-line texts are bordered as a single block. The tool calculates the longest line and pads shorter lines to match that width, ensuring uniform borders around all content.

Can I customize the border characters?

This tool uses predefined border styles. For custom borders, you can copy the output and manually edit the border characters to suit your specific design requirements.

Where are text borders commonly used?

Text borders are popular in terminal applications, ASCII art, forum signatures, documentation headers, banners, and anywhere visual separation or decoration enhances text presentation.
