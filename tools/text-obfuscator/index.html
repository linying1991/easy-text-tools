Frequently Asked Questions

What is text obfuscation?

Text obfuscation transforms readable text into a less recognizable form while preserving its meaning or structure. It's used for privacy, puzzles, creative effects, and preventing casual reading without encryption.

How does leet speak work?

Leet speak (1337) replaces letters with similar-looking numbers or symbols. Common substitutions include A→4, E→3, I→1, O→0, S→5, and T→7. It originated in early computer culture and gaming communities.

What is jumbling middle characters?

Jumbling preserves the first and last letters of each word while randomly rearranging the middle characters. Surprisingly, humans can still read this because we recognize words by their first and last letters.

When is text obfuscation useful?

Obfuscation is useful for privacy in screenshots, creating puzzles, protecting spoilers, adding creative flair to text, obscuring sensitive information in examples, and recreational wordplay.
