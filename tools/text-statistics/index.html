Frequently Asked Questions

How is reading time calculated?

Reading time is estimated at 200 words per minute, which is the average reading speed for comprehension. This may vary based on text complexity and reader skill.

How are paragraphs counted?

Paragraphs are counted as blocks of text separated by one or more blank lines. Multiple consecutive line breaks are treated as a single paragraph separator.

How are sentences counted?

Sentences are counted by identifying sentence-ending punctuation (periods, exclamation points, question marks) followed by whitespace.

Why might word count differ from other tools?

Word counting algorithms may differ in how they handle hyphens, contractions, and special characters. This tool splits text on whitespace to count words.
