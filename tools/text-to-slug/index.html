Frequently Asked Questions

What is a slug?

A slug is a URL-friendly version of a title or phrase, typically used in web addresses. It contains only lowercase letters, numbers, hyphens, and underscores, with no special characters or spaces.

Why are slugs important for SEO?

SEO-friendly slugs help search engines understand your content and make URLs more readable for users. They should accurately describe the page content and include relevant keywords.

How do I customize my slug?

You can customize your slug using the options: make lowercase, remove special characters, trim spaces, use strict mode (only alphanumeric), and choose your separator (hyphen, underscore, etc.).

What are best practices for creating slugs?

Keep them short but descriptive, use hyphens to separate words, include important keywords, avoid stop words when possible, and make sure they're readable and understandable.
